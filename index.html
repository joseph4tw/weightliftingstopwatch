About
What's this stopwatch for?

Weightlifting Stopwatch was developed to help:

  • Track overall workout time
  • Give a visual / audible queue for your rest periods

My biggest issue with using a regular stopwatch was that when I finished my set, I had to remember what time it was so that I knew when I needed to start up my next set. It got annoying to remember and keep watching the clock so I decided to make this simple app.

This is actually inspired by Michael Matthew's Bigger Leaner Stronger workouts since the rest periods are longer than what I'm used to. So far this stopwatch / rest timer combo has been a great help.

If you're curious about the workout program, click the book image.

Pleas note, I'm not affiliated with Michael Matthews or any of his businesses; I'm just a fan who made an app based on one of his products.
